About Me

Hello, I'm Kristie Cook, a dedicated web developer embarking on the exciting journey of my software engineering career. Recently completing a rigorous 15-week Tech Women of Color Software Engineering Pre-Apprenticeship Program, I proudly hold a Web Development Software Engineer certificate. My proficiency spans Java, Git, Advanced HTML/5 & CSS3, and hands-on experience with portfolio projects, encompassing both front-end frameworks like React + Redux and back-end technologies such as Node.js and Express. In addition to my technical expertise, I bring valuable certifications as an Implementation Support Specialist and Tech Software Support Specialist, showcasing my adeptness in guiding clients through software implementation and resolving technical challenges. Eager to evolve my coding prowess and project management skills, I am actively seeking immersive "learn on the job" opportunities. My commitment goes beyond technical skills; I am passionate about bringing diversity to the field of technology. Drawing from a diverse professional background that includes roles as a Life Coach, Customer Service Professional, and Project Management, I offer a unique perspective poised to contribute to innovation in the tech industry. Actively pursuing continuous education and training, I am committed to expanding my skillsets. I welcome connections through my contact link and am excited about the potential for collaboration and growth.
